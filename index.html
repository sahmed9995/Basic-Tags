This is how you bake a cake


Disclaimer: There are actually different types of cakes such as:

  • Cheesecake
  • Torte
  • Tres Leches
  • Fruit Cake

These are just some, to name a few. However for this webpage we are going to make a simple vanilla cake. A picture is provided below.

This is the recipe:
  1. Add dry ingredients including flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt
  2. Add wet ingredients in a different bowl including eggs, water, vanilla extract, and oil
  3. Combine wet with dry ingredients
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes
  5. Wait till cake cools and then refridgerate for about an hour
  6. Finally ice the cake
